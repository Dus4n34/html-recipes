Chicken Alfredo

Description

This easy slow cooker chicken Alfredo with Swiss cheese and Parmesan is super creamy and saucy. It's good served over egg noodles.

Ingredients

  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves- cubed
  • 2 jars Alfredo pasta sauce
  • 4 slices Swiss cheese
  • 3 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Stir chicken cubes and Alfredo sauce together in the crock of a slow cooker; cover, and cook on Low for 2 hours.
  3. Add Parmesan and Swiss cheese; cover and cook for another 30 minutes, or until chicken is cooked through.
  4. Season with garlic powder, salt and pepper; stir well before serving.
